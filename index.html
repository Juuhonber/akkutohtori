About Me

Dr. Juho Heiska is a distinguished expert in the field of battery technology and energy storage systems. With a robust academic background, including a D.Sc. (Tech.) and a specialized Battery MBA, Dr. Heiska has dedicated his career to advancing sustainable energy solutions. His extensive research and development work, particularly at Seinäjoki University of Applied Sciences (SEMK) and Vaasa University of Applied Sciences (VAMK), have contributed significantly to the understanding and improvement of smart grid technologies and Li-ion battery systems. A prolific educator and a passionate advocate for clean energy, Dr. Heiska’s expertise encompasses the development of Li-Organic Battery Materials with Atomic/Molecular Layer Deposition, making him a leading voice in the battery consulting arena. His commitment to innovation and sustainability is evident in his contributions to the field, positioning him as your go-to specialist for cutting-edge battery advice and consultation.
